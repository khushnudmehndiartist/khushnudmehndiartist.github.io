No. WhatsApp or email enquiry for a quote is completely free and non-binding. We aim for fast replies, usually under 1 hour*.

Once details are finalized on WhatsApp, an advance payment is required to confirm and secure your date, especially for bridal slots.
Which areas do you cover?
We offer home service across the Mumbai Metropolitan Region (MMR), including Mumbai, Navi Mumbai, and Thane. Out-station bookings are accommodated upon request.

Yes, absolutely. We only use high-quality, pure, skin-safe cones to ensure a beautiful, rich, and dark stain without any harmful chemicals.
